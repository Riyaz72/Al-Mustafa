How do I Make Booking For Health Screening Appointment?

To Get Health Screening Appointment, You Can Follow These Steps:

  • 1. Visit The Clinic: Go to Al Mustafa Medical Centre in person and inquire about their health screening programs at the reception. The staff can provide detailed information about the available screening packages and help you schedule an appointment.
  • 2. Call The Clinic: Contact Al Mustafa Medical Centre via phone. Call the clinic number +65 62090979 to speak with a representative who can guide you through the process of booking a health screening appointment.
  • 3. Online Appointment Booking: Visit Al Mustafa Medical Centre’s website, look for the "Book an Appointment" or "Health Screening" section, and follow the instructions to schedule your screening online.
  • 4. Email Inquiry: If you prefer, you can send an email to Al Mustafa Medical Centre’s official email address admin@almustafa.com.sg . In your email, mention your interest and request information on available packages and appointment availability. The clinic staff will respond with the necessary details and further instructions.
  • 5. Consult Your Primary Care Physician: If you already have a primary care physician associated with Al Mustafa Medical Centre, discuss your interest in health screening with them. They can provide recommendations on the appropriate screening packages and help you set up an appointment.

Can I Proceed For Health Screening After Vaccination?

  • It is recommended to wait for a few days after your vaccination to ensure accurate results.
  • Some vaccines might cause temporary changes in your body's immune response and health metrics.
  • If you have any concerns, it’s best to consult with your healthcare provider before scheduling your screening.
